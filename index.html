Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, a biodiversity hotspot, boasts a rich variety of flora and fauna, including many endemic species. The island features stunning landscapes like the lush Sinharaja Forest Reserve, the picturesque tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya, and the dramatic Horton Plains. Yala and Wilpattu National Parks are famous for their leopards and diverse wildlife. Cultural treasures like Sigiriya Rock Fortress and ancient cities like Anuradhapura add to its allure. Coastal gems such as Mirissa and Unawatuna offer pristine beaches and vibrant marine life, making Sri Lanka a captivating destination for nature and culture enthusiasts alike.
